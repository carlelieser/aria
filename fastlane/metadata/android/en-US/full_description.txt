Aria is a music player built with a plugin architecture, supporting multiple streaming providers including YouTube Music, Spotify, and local files.

<b>Features</b>

• <b>Plugin architecture</b> - Built-in providers for YouTube Music, Spotify, and local files
• <b>Library management</b> - Organize songs, albums, artists, and playlists
• <b>Material Design 3</b> - Dynamic theming with accent colors and light/dark/system modes
• <b>Search & discovery</b> - Search across multiple providers with filtering and sorting
• <b>Offline downloads</b> - Download music with queue management and storage tracking
• <b>Synced lyrics</b> - Line-by-line timing with plain text fallback
• <b>10-band equalizer</b> - Presets including Flat, Bass Boost, and Treble Boost
• <b>Sleep timer</b> - Presets, custom duration, and end-of-track mode
• <b>Queue management</b> - Reorder tracks, shuffle, and repeat modes
